My Classes

Outdoor Sports:  Spring 2018

This class has focused on outdoor sports, however we also have quizzes and labs that help us to learn what it means to live a healthy lifestyle. We focus on diet, phsyical anatomy of muscles, as well as mental health. This class has been benefificial for me to get into shape and to understand how the whole body is connected.

Class Objectives:

  1. Learning and playing outdoor sports
    • Flag football
    • Ultimate frisbee
    • Soccer
  2. Heath and the body
    • Diet
    • How to work out/how much is good for you
    • How the brain and body are connected
    • What it means to be healthy

An essential part of each person's life is how they take care of themselves. The way we treat our bodies and our minds inarguably affects our daily lives. I am grateful to have had the opportunity to take this class, because it has offered me tools to be able to take care of my body better, which will allow me to be a better person, worker, and friend, and hopefully to live a longer and happier life.
